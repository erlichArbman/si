What it Means to Intend Synergy:


Intend

/in'tent/

verb

1. have (a course of action) as one's purpose or objective, plan.

2. design or destine (someone or something) for a particular purpose or end.


Synergy

/ˈsinərjē/

noun

the interaction or cooperation of two or more organizations, substances, or other agents to produce a combined effect greater than the sum of their seprate effects.

Our Mission

Synergistic Intentions was founded by data management specialists who were tired of following the limiting status quo of the industry. Our primary service involves taking on the responsibility of managing data for our clients so they don't have to, thus allowing our clients to focus on what it is that they do best, while we do what we do best: manage data. However, while of course we do provide the basic services akin to any data management firm (keeping, updating, and organizing data), we set ourself apart from the competition by not stopping there. Here at Synergistic Intentions, we analyze the data of our clients to find oppurtunities for in which our clients could stand to benefit from each other. When our clients seek our services, they are gaining access to our wealth of intellectual capital as we will work to determine what oppurtunities we can leverage between our clients. Together, we will know more. Together, we will do better. Together, we are Synergistic Intentions.
